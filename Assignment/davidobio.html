DAVIDO BIOGRAPHY

Davido, born David Adedeji Adeleke on November 21, 1992, in Atlanta, Georgia, is a Nigerian singer, songwriter, and record producer. He rose to fame after the release of his hit single "Dami Duro" from his debut album Omo Baba Olowo. Davido is known for his global appeal and has collaborated with both African and international artists. Early Life and Career: Davido was born in Atlanta, Georgia, to Nigerian parents, Adedeji and Vero Adeleke. He spent his early years in Lagos, Nigeria, and attended the British International School. He initially studied Business Administration at Oakwood University (formerly Oakwood College) in Alabama before dropping out to pursue music. Davido's interest in music began at a young age, and he started producing and recording music in his late teens. He returned to Nigeria and began his music career, releasing his debut single "Back When" featuring Naeto C in 2011. His breakthrough came with the release of "Dami Duro" and the subsequent success of his debut album Omo Baba Olowo.

Musical Career and Achievements: Davido has released several successful singles and albums, including A Good Time (2019) and A Better Time (2020). He is known for hits like "Fall," "If," "Assurance," and "Blow My Mind". He founded his own record label, Davido Music Worldwide (DMW), in 2016. Davido has won numerous awards, including a BET Award for Best International Act and a MTV Europe Music Award for Best African Act. He is recognized as one of the most influential Africans and has been featured on Forbes and other international platforms.

Key Aspects Global Appeal: Davido's music has gained significant international recognition, with his songs charting in various countries and his music videos accumulating billions of views on YouTube. Record Label: DMW has signed several successful artists, showcasing Davido's entrepreneurial spirit and commitment to promoting other talents. Philanthropy: Davido has been involved in various philanthropic activities, including donations to educational institutions and support for community development. Davido's biography is a testament to his talent, hard work, and business acumen. He has successfully navigated the music industry, achieving both local and international recognition while also establishing himself as a successful entrepreneur.
